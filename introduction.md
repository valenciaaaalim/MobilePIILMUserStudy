<h1 align="center"><strong>Understanding How Privacy Warnings Prevent Disclosure of Personal Information While Messaging.</strong></h1>

This study examines how people respond to privacy warnings while typing messages. You will complete a short smartphone-based study with **three fictional WhatsApp-style conversations**, typing brief replies using provided text. Some participants will see privacy warnings while others will not. **Please complete this study using your mobile phone browser.**

The study takes about **12 minutes**. All scenarios are fictional. Participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time, with payment subject to Prolific’s standard procedures.
Thank you for participating in this research study.

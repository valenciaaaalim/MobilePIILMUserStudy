## What is the purpose of this research
The purpose of this study is to understand whether privacy warnings shown while people are typing messages can help them notice and reflect on the sharing of personal information, such as phone numbers or addresses. Although messaging apps are often encrypted, users may still accidentally share sensitive information. This research studies how easy it is for users to understand privacy warnings and how such warnings affect usability and privacy protection during messaging.

## Who can participate in the research, and what is the expected duration
You may participate if you:
- Are between 21 and 49 years old.
- Are fluent in English.
- Use a smartphone for the study.

You may not participate if you are using a desktop or non-mobile device.

Your participation will take approximately 12 minutes in a single session. The overall research study is expected to last approximately 3 months.

## What is the approximate number of participants involved
This study will involve approximately 144 participants recruited online.

## What will be done if I take part in this research
If you agree to participate in this research study, the following will occur:
- You will first complete a short online questionnaire about your familiarity with online privacy and messaging.
- You will read several fictional, preloaded messaging conversations on a secure web page and type brief replies using provided reference text. No real contacts are involved.
- Depending on the study condition you are randomly assigned to, some participants will see automated privacy-related feedback while composing their reply, which may include suggestions to reduce personal information, while others will not.
- After each conversation, you will answer a few short questions about your experience and perceptions.
- At the end of the study, you will complete a final questionnaire about your overall experience.
- You will then be redirected back to Prolific to complete the study and receive compensation.

Total participation time is approximately 12 minutes.

## How will my privacy and the confidentiality of my research records be protected
No real names, phone numbers, contact details, or personal chat histories are collected and so all responses will be anonymous. Please do not enter your own real personal details in your replies, even if the conversation asks for them. Your Prolific Participant ID will be collected, which anonymises your personal identity and is used solely for payment and study completion. Other data collected consists of the interaction logs of the experiment and your questionnaire responses during the experiment.

Only the principal investigator and co-investigators will have access to the data collected in this research. The data will be password protected and stored in a secure server in SUTD. Results will be reported in aggregate form, and no individual participant will be identifiable in any publication or presentation.
